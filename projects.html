Disney Ticket Queues Simulation

Systems Simulation - Spring 2020

This was a group project where we created a simulation of the Animal Kingdom ticket lines and park entrance using Simio. We gathered data from time studies of the entry lines and used this information to determine the optimal layout to reduce queueing times.

Examination of the Opioid Epidemic

Decision Support Systems - Fall 2019

Using Python and Anaconda, myself and a group of 3 other members conducted a statistical analysis of how the opioid epidemic affected Broward County and Orange County differently. We used various Python tools, focusing on Scipy for the statistics testing and Pyplot for the data visualization.
